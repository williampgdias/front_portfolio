Our Team:

We are a group of friends from Brazil and we are trying to give a different ideia that how food made 100% plant based (nothing that involve ingredient from any part of any animal) can be a complete, easy and delicious meal.

Our chef William started his adventure when he went to Dublin-Ireland in 2013 to study English. He started his journey in the kitchen in 2015 washing dishes, but always looking into the prep kitchen dreaming doing that for life. When he started as kitchen assistant, 3 months later, he saw something completely different. His life was really hard professionally, when many different thoughts happened. He started to change his mind and what he was doing in the planet.

Menu:

The Chef decided to create this incredible menu to show everyone that we do not need complex ingredients to create a delicious dish.
He believes that everyone can eat a proper meal and enjoy what the earth gave to us. You are going to see a different perspective of cooking.

Chickpea Stroganoff

I know what you're thinking. Stroganoff with chickpeas? What?? Excuse me?? Really?? Hmm hmhm... Actually, we used to think that too, but when we tried the first time, wow!
It is definetely a must try option dish from this menu, as you will enjoy a incredible stroganoff with chickpeas in a home-made tomato sauce and soya fresh cream. It comes with white rice and baked potato wedges.
(Gluten free)

Spaguetti alla Bolognese

The name of the dish say everything that we need to know. Pasta with bolognese. That is all we need No words to describe the taste of this rich bolognese home-made tomato sauce with red and yellow peppers, fried with garlic, onion and the favorite top list of herbs and spice of the chef.
(Gluten free option available)

Not Beef Lasagna

You cannot wait to try this piece of magic dish. Layered with a combination of a 100% plant based mince meat, in a rich home-made tomato and white sauce, with variety of vegetables, and finished with Almond parmesan. Served with Rocket salad and coleslaw.
(Contain: nuts)
